Visual Media

Saipain Suicides

The video shows the aftermath of the mass suicides on Saipan Islands. The images convey how the war had adverse effects on civilian populations, and an invasion might have provoked more mass suicides.

Paul Tibbets on the Atomic Bomb

Paul Tibbets, the pilot of the Enola Gay, describes the sheer force and power of the atomic bomb. Tibbets’s description shows how the use of the atomic bomb was a great display of force of the American weapons arsenal to Stalin and the Soviet Union.

Japanese Surrender

This is a video of Japan signing the final surrender to the United States on the USS Missouri on 2 September 1945. This shows how quickly the atomic bomb was able to end the war, considering the road to the capital would have been a long and bloody one. images

Truman on the Atomic Bomb

In the video, President Truman communicates the political and military motives to develop and use the atomic bombs. Truman states that the power of the weapons will lead to a quick end to the war, and the US is building an arsenal far superior to any other nation in the world.
